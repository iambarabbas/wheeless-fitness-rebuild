My Story

Helping Men Over 50 Stay Strong for Life

Functional fitness is the cornerstone of everything I do. My goal is simple: help you live a long, healthy, productive life—enabling your body to do anything and everything well into your 70s, 80s, and beyond.

I've spent over 15 years working with men in San Diego's North County who refuse to let age define them. My clients are golfers, tennis players, skiers, and outdoor enthusiasts who want to keep doing what they love—just better.

I understand the unique challenges men face in their 50s and 60s. Your body responds differently than it did at 30, and your training should reflect that. I design programs that build sustainable strength, protect your joints, and enhance performance in the sports that matter to you.
